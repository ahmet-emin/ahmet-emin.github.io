Featured Project

Color Match

Color Match redefines classic merge mechanics with a dynamic physics-based aiming system, creating a unique hybrid strategy experience. Players must master the "Smart Lock" mechanic to hunt moving targets, managing a high-stakes risk/reward balance to trigger Mega Explosions within milliseconds. Beyond a simple test of reflexes, the game offers a creative building journey; every victory powers a satisfying, ASMR-focused meta-game where players bring dormant, gray worlds back to life using a stunning "Selective Saturation" aesthetic.
